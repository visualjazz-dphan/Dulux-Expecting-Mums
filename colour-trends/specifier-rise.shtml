Dulux Colour Forecast 2013

Collaborations between Japanese and Western designers and manufacturers are creating products that are innovative, whilst reinterpreting forms and styles that are quintessentially Japanese.

Somewhat austere, the Japanese design aesthetic is calm, quiet, considered and above all functional – a design style that is fitting with today’s need for a cocooning, composed environment and a space for contemplation. A palette of sophisticated pastels allows otherwise bulky forms to appear light and even soft
