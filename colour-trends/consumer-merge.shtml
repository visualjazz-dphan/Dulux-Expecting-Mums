Merge

Merge evokes nostalgia by merging past, present & future styles for something entirely new but familiar. Craftsmanship, reconciling analogue with digital & simple processes with innovative solutions inform the Merge trend.

Tradition is reflected in the palette where materials associated with craftsmanship are represented in hues of stone, timber & leather while natural dying processes inspire red hues and orange based pinks. See our ‘get the look’ tips below for easy ways to bring the Merge trend into your home.

Want to recreate the Merge look in your home? Here’s some tips on adding some exciting Dulux colour to your interior

  • Use the Merge palette’s orange based pinks on a feature wall or in décor details to give traditional looks a new twist
  • A stoney coloured neutral like Dulux Linseed is a great start in creating the Merge look. To add warmth utilise other colours in décor details
  • Give a nod to traditional and introduce mid century inspired furniture pieces in warm mid toned timber finishes
