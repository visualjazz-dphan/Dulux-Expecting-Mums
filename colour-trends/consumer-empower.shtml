Empowered by communication literally at our fingertips, ‘people power’ is reinvented through digital connections and social media. Empower is a palette that captures this movement and its spirit with a range of blues inspiring optimism, openness and communication.

Layering Blues demonstrates the power of colour en masse while creating a vibrant refreshing space. Colour blocking is key to make a powerful on-trend statement in an entry, living area or bedroom. See our ‘get the look’ tips below for easy ways to bring the Empower trend into your home.

Want to recreate the Empower look in your home? Here’s some tips on adding some exciting Dulux colour to your interior

  • Try colour blocking using one colour on the walls, one for the ceiling & one as a feature for maximum effect
  • Dip dye or ombre looks are hot in 2013, why not have a go at creating them with paint using your three favourite Empower colours
  • Add elements of the Empower trend to a neutral interior by painting a feature wall or door, completing the look with layers of blue in other décor
