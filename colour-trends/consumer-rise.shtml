Rise

Dulux Colour Forecast 2013

Somewhat austere, the Japanese design aesthetic is calm, quiet, considered and above all functional – a design style that is fitting with today’s need for a cocooning, composed environment and a space for contemplation.

Sooth your senses with the almost ethereal soft shades of Rise, a palette of sophisticated pastels that allows otherwise bulky forms to appear light and even soft. The perfect palette for any interior space, see our ‘get the look’ tips below for easy ways to bring the Rise trend into your home.

Want to recreate the Rise look in your home? Here’s some tips on adding some exciting Dulux colour to your interior

  • Create your own sanctuary with soft warm neutrals from the Rise palette, accented with icy gelato hues such as Focus Point, Dewpoint Quarter and Celery Satin
  • Using paint, stencil simple origami inspired shapes on your wall, creating a focus point or use these forms to inspire your furniture selection
  • Rise celebrates simple beauty - keep furniture finishes natural or matt, timber finishes are washed out, naturally pale or limed
  • Patterns in the Rise trend are created by textures such as folds, pleats and laser cuts
