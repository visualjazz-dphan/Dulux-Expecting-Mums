Barrier Technology™

Our latest polymer technology combined with high performance pigments forms a tough, harder wearing barrier that most water or oil based stains cannot penetrate. This makes cleaning remarkably easy and free of shiny marks. Which means your walls are protected against even the toughest wear and tear.

Dulux Wash & Wear® is Australian made and owned, plus it is a Dulux Eco Choice® paint^

^Wash & Wear® Semi Gloss, Low Sheen and Flat bases are Eco Choice paints (tinted and untinted). Dulux Eco Choice paints conform to the relevant Green Building Council of Australia standards for VOC content. Note that Wash & Wear® Gloss meets the Green Building Council standard in the trim category at < 75g per litre of VOCs.
Polymer technology forms a barrier that protects against most oil and water based stains. Large and small pigment particles embedded in the polymer reinforce the barrier.
